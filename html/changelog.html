31 March 2021

SkyratBot updated:

  • The flying damage slowdown movetype no longer affects floating humanoids rather than flying ones.
  • Fixes runtime in turf destruction that could cause transparent turfs to render incorrectly.
  • Lowered the chance of disease creation by ~60%
  • Adds eigenstasium, a new reagent that uses the new mechanics in depth!
  • Fixed a few things to do with reactions on the backend
  • refactors status effects to have a var for processing speed
  • The fork has a new sprite.

  • Securicat plush!!
